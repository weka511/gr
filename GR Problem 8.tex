\begin{abstract}
	
Show that $\hbar^2 \Box e^{-i\frac{\Psi}{hbar}}=0$ reduces to $g^{\mu\nu}\partial_{\mu}\Psi \partial_{\nu}\Psi=0$ as $\hbar \rightarrow 0$, where $\Box = \frac{1}{\sqrt(|g|)} \partial_{\mu} g^{\mu\nu}\sqrt(|g|)\partial_{\nu}$.

\end{abstract}
